Samvrutha Sunil commonly known as Samvrutha is a former Indian movie actress who predominantly starred in Malayalam movies. She acted in the movies titled Arabikkatha, Chocolate, Thirakkatha, Boomi Malayalam, Cocktail, Swapna Sanchari, Manikyakkallu, Arike, Diamond Necklac and Ayalum Njanum Thammil.
