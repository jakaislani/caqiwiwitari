John Cena Breaks His Nose on RAW

During an exchange of blows on last night's RAW, Seth Rollins kneed John Cena in the face, breaking his nose. While Cena was clearly in pain and stunned by the blow, he managed to finish the match, as the two put on a great main event.

Cena's nose sure didn't look pretty afterwards, as photos taken backstage show a crooked nose. Knowing John Cena, this won't keep him out of action and he'll likely be wrestling his matches for the next few weeks in a protective mask. (Hey, new gimmick idea!) Finishing a match with a broken nose has been a standard practice in wrestling forever... But of course, it's all fake.
