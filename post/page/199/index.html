Who invented colour camera?

published on May 22, 2024
The first properly usable and commercially successful screen process—the autochrome—was invented early in the 20th century by two French brothers, Auguste and Louis Lumière. When was the colored camera invented? The first commercially successful color photography process appeared on the market in 1907, when the French Lumière brothers, by then famous in the world of cinema, introduced the Lumière Autochrome. Who invented the three color camera? Danish-American inventor August Plahn built and patented a camera and projector that split motion picture images through three color lenses using 70mm film.
