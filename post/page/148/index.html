The Exterior of the Streacom F12C Desktop Aluminum Case

published on June 18, 2024
The Streacom F12C is clearly a minimalistic design. It highlights the simplicity of plain sandblasted aluminum, with the only aesthetic touches being the rounded corners and bottom edge of the faceplate. The F12C features a simple yet elegant appearance. Measuring 184 × 440 × 320 mm (7.25 × 17.35 × 12.6 in) and with a volume of 0.0259 m3 (25.9 liters), the F12C is one of the largest desktop cases that we have ever seen, yet it still requires about 50% less space than an average tower case. WWE Announces Gasoline By The Weeknd As WrestleMania 40 Theme Song

published on June 18, 2024
The Weeknd continues to be a big part of WrestleMania. In a tweet, WWE announced that The Weeknd will have an official WrestleMania theme song for the fifth year in a row. In 2024, “Gasoline” will be an official theme song of WrestleMania 40. Blinding Lights” was the theme song of WrestleMania 36, while The Weeknd went back-to-back with “Save Your Tears” for WrestleMania 37.
