Although the band has gone through a number of lineup changes over the years, its core configuration of Haynes, Leary, and drummer King Coffey has been constant since 198Teresa Nervosa was the second drummer for the band from 1983 to 1985, 1986 to 1989, and again in 200The band has also had a number of different bass players, the most notable of whom being Jeff Pinkus. Having their origins in the 1980s hardcore punk scene, the Butthole Surfers quickly gained notoriety for their frantic and disturbing live shows as well as their black comedy and an eclectic musical style that incorporated elements of psychedelic and noise music with punk rock and electronica elements later on, as well as their use of sound manipulation and tape editing. Butthole Surfers are known to have a voracious hunger for recreational drugs, which has a noticeable impact on their sound.
