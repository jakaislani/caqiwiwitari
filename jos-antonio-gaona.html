Biography

José Antonio Gaona was born in Mexico on April 19, 1982. Mexican actor who is known for his work in television, theater, and film. He is best recognized for his recurring television appearances including as Emilio Echegaray in Rosario Tijeras, Javier Giraldo in Palabra de Ladron, and Yoni in Campeones de la Vida. He starred alongside Ana Serradilla and Ana Layevska in the 2006 feature film Cansada de Besar Sapos. He graduated from the CasAzul Escuela de Artes Escenicas and started his career as an actor on television. In 2003, he made his debut as the character Cocho in the series El Alma Herida. He then went on to make his feature film debut in 2006 when he starred as Tizoc in the film Cansada de Besar Sapos.
