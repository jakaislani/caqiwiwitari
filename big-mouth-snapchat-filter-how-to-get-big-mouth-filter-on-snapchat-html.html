Big Mouth Snapchat Filter : How to get big mouth filter on Snapchat?

Do you want to make funny videos with the Big Mouth Snapchat filter? In this article, we’ll guide you on how to access this entertaining filter and add a touch of humor to your Snapchat videos.

Accessing the Big Mouth Snapchat Filter The Big Mouth Snapchat filter is a popular feature that lets you create videos with an enlarged mouth effect. To get started, make sure you have the Snapchat app installed on your device. If you don’t have it yet, you can download it from your app store.

Steps to Use the Big Mouth Snapchat Filter

  • Open Snapchat: Launch the Snapchat app on your device.
  • Access Filters: When you’re in camera mode, you’ll see a circular icon that looks like a smiley face on the right side of the screen. Tap this icon to access available filters.
  • Browse Filters: Swipe through the filters until you find the “Big Mouth” filter. It might be in different sections like trending, face effects, or special filters.
  • Apply Big Mouth Filter: Once you find the Big Mouth filter, tap it to apply it to your face in the camera preview.
  • Record Your Video: After applying the filter, press and hold the record button (the red circle) at the bottom center of the screen to start recording your video. The Big Mouth filter effect will be active while you’re holding the button.
  • Get Creative: While recording, you’ll see the enlarged mouth effect on your face in real-time. Have fun by talking, making expressions, or doing anything that enhances the filter’s effect.
  • Finish Recording: Release the record button when you’re done recording your video. The video will automatically save to your device.
  • Review and Share: Take a moment to review your video and make any desired edits. Once you’re satisfied, you can share the video with your friends by selecting the “Send To” button.
