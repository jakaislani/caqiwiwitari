Kam Chancellor was born in Norfolk, VA on April 3, 1988. Strong safety who was named to his first Pro Bowl with the Seattle Seahawks in 2011 and helped lead the Seahawks to victory in Super Bowl XLVIII. He joined Richard Sherman and Earl Thomas in helping the Seahawks’ defense earn the nickname The Legion of Boom.
