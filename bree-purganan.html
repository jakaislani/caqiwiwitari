A Little About Jalen Green

Jalen Green, or Jalen Romande Green, was born on February 9, 2022, in Merced, California. He is a professional NBA player of Filipino descent, making him third in the line behind Raymond Townsend and Jordan Clarkson.

Jalen was already been playing basketball since sixth grade. Back then, he played for Amateur Athletic Union and practiced at least five hours daily. This proves that working hard will be fruitful.

Jalen Helped Win The Championship When He Was In High School

Jalen played for San Joaquin Memorial High School in his three high school years. When he was just in his junior year, he led the team to the California Interscholastic Federation's Division II quarterfinals.

Jalen led his team to win the CIF Division II title in his second year. Similarly, he got the second CIF Division II title consecutively. After that, he was transferred to Prolific Prep, where he led the team to win the Grind Session World Championship. He was named the Grind Session's most valuable player along with Daishen Nix in 2020.

Bree Purganan Son Jalen Green Has Three Gold Medals Under His Belt

Jalen plays for the US internationally, making his debut on the world stage at FIBA Under-16 Americas Championship in 2017. There he got his first gold medal with his team.

In the 2018 FIBA Under -17 Basketball World Cup, he was awarded the MVP in the tournament, winning his second gold medal. Similarly, he got his third gold medal in the FIBA Under-19 Basketball World Cup in 2019, held in Greece.
