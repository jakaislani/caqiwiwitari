Max Aaron (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Comedic YouTube personality who stars in his videos as characters simply known as Psycho Kid and Fat Kid. He has earned more than 290,000 subscribers to his CrizRaging channel.

Before Fame

He ran several different YouTube channels as far back as the age of nine, but most of them got taken down. 

Trivia

His first video to reach 1 million views was "Psycho kid rages over Minecraft."

Family Life

He's featured his pet Golden Retriever on his Instagram account.

Associated With

In his The CrizRaging Podcast, he has discussed the likes of web star Jacob Sartorius and President Donald Trump.
